# Timeline & Investment

## Delivery Timeline

| Phase | Deliverable | Timeframe |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Phase 1 — Core Engine** | Data extraction, employee matching, roster vs actual comparison, shift-level flagging, basic results view | Weeks 1–3 |
| **Phase 2 — Dashboard & Reporting** | Wage % dashboard, location breakdown, revenue input, Excel export | Weeks 3–4 |
| **Phase 3 — Trends & Polish** | Multi-period trends, configurable thresholds, persistent offender detection, UI refinements | Weeks 4–5 |

**Total estimated delivery: 4–5 weeks**

All three phases will be delivered together as a complete solution.
