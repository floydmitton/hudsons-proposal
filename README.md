# Hudson's Dry Cleaning

**Prepared by:** Nine Advisory\
**Date:** 25 March 2026\
**Version:** 1.0

---

Hudson's Dry Cleaning operates multiple locations across Melbourne with approximately 60 employees. This proposal outlines a purpose-built monitoring tool to compare **rostered labour** against **actual timesheets and payslips** — giving you clear visibility into where wage costs are drifting from plan, by how much, and why.

This tool is an extension of Nine Advisory's existing payroll monitoring platform, ensuring a fast build and seamless experience.

---

### What You'll Get

Each pay period, upload three files (payslips, roster, timesheets) and within minutes receive:

- **A clear view of where your roster and actuals diverge** — by employee, by location, by shift
- **Dollar figures on every variance** — _"this employee cost $X more than rostered because of Y"_
- **Wage % tracking** — know exactly where you stand relative to your target each period
- **Actionable flags** — specific issues to raise with staff
- **Exportable reports** — Excel files you can share with management or use in team meetings
